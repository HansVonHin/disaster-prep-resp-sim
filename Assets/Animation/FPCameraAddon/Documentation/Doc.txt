vFirstPerson.CS
***********************

1 - drop the inVector Controller prefab on your scene and add this script on it.
2 - assign the camera gameobject child of vThirdPersonCamera into the MainCamera of this script in the editor
3 - Have fun! Adjust settings to your needs.
3.1 - OPTIONAL: There is a FP@CameraState profile added in Prefabs > Camera. You can use this as a base for your camera settings.
